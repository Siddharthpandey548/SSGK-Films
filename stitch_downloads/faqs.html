Navigating Your Story

Clarity is the foundation of a masterpiece. Below you will find the answers to our most common inquiries regarding our process, style, and international engagements.

What is the Cinematic Legacy style? keyboard_arrow_down
Our Cinematic Legacy style goes beyond traditional event photography. We treat your life milestones as scenes in a grand film, utilizing dramatic lighting, editorial composition, and thoughtful direction to create visual heirlooms that possess the depth and gravitas of motion pictures.
Do you offer international services? keyboard_arrow_down
Yes. L'Élite Cinéma frequently travels globally to document exclusive events. International commissions require advanced booking and are subject to custom logistical arrangements to ensure our entire suite of equipment and personnel can be accommodated seamlessly.
How long is the post-production process? keyboard_arrow_down
True art cannot be rushed. Our meticulous post-production process, which includes individualized color grading, spatial retouching, and narrative sequencing, typically requires 8 to 12 weeks following your event. A preview collection is generally provided within the first 14 days.
Are raw files provided? keyboard_arrow_down
To maintain the integrity of the L'Élite Cinéma aesthetic, we do not release raw, unedited files. The cinematic look you are commissioning us for is heavily dependent on our proprietary post-production techniques. You will receive a fully curated and finalized masterpiece.
How do we begin the booking process? keyboard_arrow_down
The process begins with an initial consultation, either in person at our studio or via private video conference. This allows us to understand the scope of your narrative. Following this, a custom proposal and creative brief will be presented for your review.
